Both. The intake form and scoring rubric work for anyone. The governance checklist and comms templates are written in plain English.

**Does this cover specific AI tools?**
No. This is tool-agnostic. It's the operating model — the process, governance, and communication — not the technology selection.

**Can I edit the templates?**
Yes. You get editable DOCX and XLSX files. Brand them, modify them, make them yours.
