### FAQ

**Is this Copilot-specific or generic AI comms?**
Copilot-specific. References Microsoft 365, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, Teams. But the structure works for any AI rollout with minor edits.

**Can I brand these with our company name and logo?**
Yes. Every file is editable DOCX and PPTX. Add your logo, colors, company name.

**Does this include technical setup guides?**
No. This is the communications and change management layer. For technical setup, use Microsoft's official Copilot deployment guide.

**Our org is still in pilot. Does this help?**
Yes. The templates include pilot-specific language. Swap "organization-wide" for "pilot group" and you're set.
