## Weekly Issue Structure
```
AGENTIC AI WEEKLY — [Date]

1. SIGNAL (1-2 sentences each, 5 items)
   - Most important agentic AI developments this week
   - What enablement leaders need to know

2. ONE DEEP DIVE (150-200 words)
   - Single analysis of the most impactful development
   - Why it matters for enterprise deployment

3. TOOL SPOTLIGHT (50-75 words)
   - One tool worth knowing about
   - What it does, who it's for, pricing

4. COMPLIANCE CORNER (1-2 sentences)
   - Any regulatory deadline or policy update
   - Action item or awareness item

5. THE NUMBERS (1 line each)
   - Interesting data points: adoption rates, cost stats, benchmarks
```
