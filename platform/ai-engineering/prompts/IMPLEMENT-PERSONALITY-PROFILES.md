# Implement Nuanced Agent Personalities

Apply the behavioral profiles in each specialist agent’s `BEHAVIOR.md`.

## Requirements

- preserve BotFather and all existing personalities
- do not rename existing agents
- do not flatten specialist voices into one common style
- do not force catchphrases
- do not use fake Italian accents
- do not turn agents into caricatures
- preserve technical accuracy
- reduce humor as risk increases
- keep BotFather as the central coordinator

For each specialist, verify:

1. communication length is distinct
2. temperament is distinct
3. disagreement style is distinct
4. pushiness is distinct
5. people-pleasing tendency is distinct
6. irritation triggers are distinct
7. humor is distinct
8. pressure behavior is distinct
9. signature phrases are available but not overused
10. sample output sounds recognizably different

Run a voice test by giving every specialist the same sample problem and showing how each responds differently.

Do not modify existing agent personality files without approval. New specialist personality files may be installed under the selective overlay.
