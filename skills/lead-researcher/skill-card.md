## Description: <br>
Automated lead research and enrichment for B2B sales. Finds companies matching your criteria, enriches with contact data, and generates personalized outreach messages. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External sales teams, agencies, consultants, freelancers, and SaaS operators use this skill to find B2B prospects matching target industries, pain points, locations, and lead counts, then prepare enriched lead records and personalized outreach drafts. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Lead research and enrichment can involve personal or business contact data and may create privacy, consent, or anti-spam compliance obligations. <br>
Mitigation: Use scoped search and enrichment provider credentials, avoid sensitive personal data, verify contact data before use, and ensure outreach complies with applicable privacy and anti-spam rules. <br>
Risk: Searching social or professional platforms can conflict with platform terms if implemented through unauthorized scraping. <br>
Mitigation: Use approved search or enrichment APIs and review provider terms before collecting or using results. <br>
Risk: Generated lead scores and outreach drafts can be inaccurate or misleading if the source signal is stale or ambiguous. <br>
Mitigation: Review sources and lead records before outreach, and treat drafted messages as editable starting points. <br>
